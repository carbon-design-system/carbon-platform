## Step 1: Learn about Carbon

Carbon translates the [IBM Design Language](http://www.ibm.com/design/language) into resources for
building digital experiences. Take the time to read the site so that you fully understand what
drives IBM’s design philosophy and principles, and can make informed decisions in your product work.

By adopting Carbon, front-end developers and engineers gain a collection of reusable components to
build websites and user interfaces quickly and efficiently.

### Understand the ecosystem

The following diagram shows the relationships of assets and guidance across IBM. The Carbon Design
System is often extended or complimented by additional product and digital systems.

## Step 2: Get Started

### Choose your framework

The Carbon Design System is built React first, with official support for Web Components. We also
support core parts of the system in vanilla JS, Angular, Vue, and Svelte—and we offer guidelines for
other frameworks.

Head over to [Libraries](/assets/libraries) to complete the installation tutorial for your framework
of choice.

System. We’ll teach you the ins and outs of using Carbon components, and introduce web development
best practices along the way. Everyone who completes the tutorial will earn an IBM digital badge.
